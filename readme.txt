This plugin gets your WooCommerce products ready to be imported by Vleks.com!

== Description ==
Instantly connect your products on the marketplaces you want to sell your products on.<br />
One single connection between Vleks and your e-commerce platform or ERP sytem will allow you to optimize your products for multiple channels and will allow you to automatically update your product prices to be competitive with other sellers.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `vleks-woocommerce` directory to the `wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress.
1. Configure your Vleks.com credentials and other options in the WooCommerce Integration Settings.
